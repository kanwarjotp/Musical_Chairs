Musical Chairs

The Story of the Magical Musical Chairs

Long ago in a Nordic Kingdom, the trees used to move to the tunes of music, living in peace with the music loving vikings. The furniture made from timber of this magical and musical land would move toward Music. Chairs used to gather around music and dance while the vikings danced around them, One night aftermuch merry making one chair, Alice forgot to go back and was left outside. You have to help the chair by guiding her through the maze always following the music.

How to Play:

Objective: Guide Alice, the musical chair to the music safely.

  • Use arrow keys to move the chair
  • Avoid Fire at all costs, it will burn Alice.
  • You can Pause the game using the ESC key

    • Tip: Be mindful of the timing of the fires and dodge them so.
